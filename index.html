Ezequiel López-López

Computational social scientist studying GenAI-mediated information ecosystems and epistemic risks — how generative AI reshapes belief formation, misinformation, and public knowledge.

Research

My work focuses on the epistemic risks introduced as generative AI becomes a central mediator of digital information flows. I study how LLMs influence information-seeking, narrative dynamics, communication patterns, and misinformation, with a focus on belief formation and public knowledge.
